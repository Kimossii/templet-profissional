120-Minute Session on Robotic Colorectal Cancer Surgery with Semi-Live Surgery

5 de fevereiro de 2025

I Portuguese Conference on Robotic Surgery

04 e 05 Jul 2025

Conference Centre Pestana Palace, Lisboa


Join us for a unparalleled session dedicated to robotic surgery in colorectal cancer, with a focus on rectal cancer and right-sided colon cancer. Organized by the Portuguese Association for Robotic Surgery (APCiR), this 120-minute session will showcase some of the world’s most esteemed colorectal surgeons.

The program will emphasize two critical aspects of robotic colorectal surgery: achieving proper oncologic dissection and ensuring safe anastomosis creation using the robotic platform. Attendees will gain invaluable insights through:


Theoretical Lectures

World-leading experts will share the latest advancements and best practices in robotic oncologic surgery.


Tips & Tricks

Practical guidance to refine surgical techniques for optimal outcomes.


﻿Semi-Live Surgery

A unique opportunity to analyze and discuss surgical procedures in detail, focusing on precision and safety.

This comprehensive session is designed to provide surgeons with cutting-edge knowledge and the skills needed to deliver the highest standards of care in robotic colorectal cancer surgery. Don’t miss this essential event!

Oesophagogastric surgery will be featured in a 120-minute session

4 de fevereiro de 2025
I Portuguese Conference on Robotic Surgery 04 e 05 Jul 2025 Conference Centre Pestana Palace, Lisboa In the first congress of the Portuguese Association for Robotic Surgery (APCIR), oesophagogastric surgery will be featured in a 120-minute session. This session, entirely dedicated to oesophageal and gastric surgery, will host renowned national and international specialists, recognized leaders in the use of robotic surgery for the treatment of these conditions. The programme will emphasise critical aspects of the approach to these pathologies, namely: Oesophageal cancer surgery - Performing intrathoracic anastomosis using the robotic platform in oesophageal cancer surgery, optimising lymphadenectomy, and proper patient positioning and docking with the available robotic platforms. Hiatal hernia - Tips & tricks for the management of hiatal hernia. ﻿ Gastric cancer surgery - Optimising lymphadenectomy in gastric cancer surgery and oesophagojejunal anastomosis in total gastrectomy. We look forward to welcoming you to this session, confident that the invited specialists will share their ideas and experiences to ensure the highest standards in using robotic platforms for the treatment of these complex conditions.

120-Minute Session «Robotic Surgical Repairs in Abdominal Wall Hernia»

3 de fevereiro de 2025
I Portuguese Conference on Robotic Surgery 04 e 05 Jul 2025 Conference Centre Pestana Palace, Lisboa Join us for a session dedicated to the ongoing revolution of robotic surgery in abdominal wall reconstruction with a focus from inguinal, umbilical with associated diastasis and complex hernias that were usually reserved for open approaches. Organized by the Portuguese Association for Robotic Surgery (APCiR), this 120-minute session will showcase some of the world’s best Abdominal Wall Reconstruction (AWR) surgeons. The program will enhance critical aspects of Robotic AWR surgery: Attendees will gain incredible insights through: Theoretical Lectures: World-leading experts will share the latest advancements and best practices in robotic surgery. Tips & Tricks: Practical guidance to refine surgical approaches for optimal outcomes. Critical review of various surgical approaches: A unique opportunity to analyze and discuss surgical procedures in detail, focusing on precision and safety. This will be an interactive session designed to provide surgeons with innovative knowledge to deliver the best standards of care in robotic AWR Surgery.
